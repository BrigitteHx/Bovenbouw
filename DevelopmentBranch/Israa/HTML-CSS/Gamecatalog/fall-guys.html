Fall Guys

Fall Guys is a multiplayer party game developed by Mediatonic and published by Devolver Digital. It features colorful, jellybean-like characters competing in various obstacle courses and mini-games in a battle royale-style format.

The goal is to outlast and outmaneuver opponents through teamwork, skill, and a bit of luck, aiming to be the last bean standing and claim victory. With its charming visuals, chaotic gameplay, and fun challenges, Fall Guys has become a popular title for both casual and competitive gamers alike.
